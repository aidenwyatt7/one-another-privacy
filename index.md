# Privacy Policy for One Another

**Last updated:** July 8, 2026

## Introduction

One Another ("the App") is a prayer and communication app for church congregations. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

The App is currently operated by **Aiden Dixon** ("we," "us"), the developer, as an independent project made available to partnering churches. If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact:

**Aiden Dixon**
aidenwyattd7@gmail.com

This policy applies to the App during its testing and pilot period. If operation of the App transfers to a church or other organization in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

---

## Minimum Age

**You must be at least 13 years old to use One Another.** The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

Users between 13 and 17 may use the App. To protect younger users, the App restricts private one-to-one messaging between adults (18+) and users 16 and under. Users 16 and under are also not listed in the church member directory.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide

- **Account information:** your name, email address, and date of birth.
- **Profile information:** an optional profile photo, and display preferences.
- **Church affiliation:** the church you join, and any groups within it. Because this App is designed for church use, your use of it indicates a religious affiliation. This is sensitive information and we treat it accordingly.
- **Prayer requests:** the content of prayer requests you submit, including any personal details you choose to share. **Prayer requests may contain sensitive information** — for example, about health, mental health, family circumstances, relationships, or personal struggles. You choose what to share, and you may submit requests anonymously.
- **Messages and encouragements:** the content of direct messages, group messages, and encouragements you send.
- **Reports:** if you report content or another user, we collect the report and a copy of the reported content.

### Information collected automatically

- **Usage information:** when you last opened the App, when you prayed for a request, and similar activity used to make the App work and to show your church aggregate participation statistics.
- **Device tokens:** an identifier from Apple or Google that allows us to send you push notifications, if you enable them.

We do not use advertising trackers, and we do not sell your information.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and maintain your account;
- Show you prayer requests to pray for, and show your requests to others in your church;
- Deliver messages, encouragements, and notifications;
- Enable prayer events, groups, and the member directory;
- Show church leadership **aggregate, church-wide** statistics (for example, how many prayers were prayed this month). These statistics never identify or rank individual members;
- Enforce safety features, review reports, and respond to misuse;
- Fix problems and improve the App.

---

## Who Can See Your Information

**Other members of your church** can see: your name and profile photo (unless you are 16 or under, in which case you do not appear in the directory), prayer requests you submit non-anonymously, and messages you send them.

**Anonymous prayer requests** do not display your name to other members. Your identity is still stored in our database so the App can function, but it is not shown to other users.

**Church leadership (moderators and pastoral team)** can see:
- All members of the church, including their names and roles;
- Members of all groups within the church, including private groups;
- Aggregate church statistics (never individual rankings);
- **Content that has been reported to them.** If you or someone else reports a message, conversation, prayer request, or user, church leadership can review the reported content — including, in the case of a reported conversation, the full message history of that conversation. This is how we keep the community safe. Church leadership cannot browse private conversations that have not been reported.

**We (the developer)** can access data stored in the App's database in order to operate, debug, and maintain the App.

We do not share your information with any other third parties except the service providers listed below, or where required by law.

---

## Service Providers

We use the following providers to operate the App. Your data is stored on their infrastructure:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and file storage. Data is hosted in the United States (US East).
- **Google Firebase (Firebase Cloud Messaging)** — push notification delivery. Firebase receives your device token and the content of notifications sent to you.
- **Apple** — App distribution (App Store / TestFlight) and push notification delivery.

These providers process data on our behalf. All data is stored in the United States.

---

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, we send push notifications about prayer, messages, and church activity. You can turn off individual notification categories in the App's settings, and you can disable notifications entirely in your device settings.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information while your account is active.

- **Prayer requests** may expire automatically after a set period, and you may close or delete your own requests.
- **Prayer event requests** are temporary by default and are not saved unless you choose to save them.
- **Messages and encouragements** are retained so that you and the recipient can continue to see them.
- **Reports** are retained so church leadership can review them and so we have a record of safety issues.

**You can delete your account from within the App.** Deleting your account removes your profile and disconnects you from your church and groups. Some information may remain where it is necessary — for example, messages you sent may still be visible to their recipients, and reports about your content may be retained for safety purposes.

You may also contact us at aidenwyattd7@gmail.com to request deletion of your data.

---

## Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and database access controls that limit what each user can see. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Please be thoughtful about what you share. Prayer requests and messages are visible to other people in your church.

---

## Your Choices

- You can edit or delete your profile information in the App.
- You can submit prayer requests anonymously.
- You can block another member, which prevents messages between you and removes you from each other's prayer matching.
- You can report content or users to church leadership.
- You can turn notifications on or off by category.
- You can delete your account.
